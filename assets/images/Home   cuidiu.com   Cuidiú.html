Parent to Parent Support

Cuidiú’s aim is to support parents in their role in order to help make parenthood the joy it should be. Our Parent to Parent supporters are willing to provide a listening ear and support for anyone who would like to talk.

Breastfeeding

Cuidiu hosts breastfeeding support groups and coffee mornings and we have almost 100 fully qualified voluntary breastfeeding counsellors who offer telephone support throughout the country.
